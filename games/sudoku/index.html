Sudoku

▶ How to play

Goal: fill every empty cell in the 9×9 grid with a digit from 1 to 9, following three rules:

1
Every row (horizontal line of 9 cells) must contain each of the digits 1–9 exactly once.
2
Every column (vertical line of 9 cells) must contain each of the digits 1–9 exactly once.
3
Every 3×3 box (the nine bold-bordered squares) must also contain each digit 1–9 exactly once.

How to enter a number: tap a white cell to select it (it highlights), then tap a digit on the number pad below. Tap ⌫ to erase. You can also use the keyboard — arrow keys to move, 1–9 to fill, Delete to erase.

Given cells (shown in cyan) are part of the puzzle — they can't be changed. Red cells mean your digit conflicts with another in the same row, column or box.

Hint reveals one cell that logic alone can determine. New to Sudoku? Start on Easy — the puzzle has more given cells and is straightforward to solve without guessing.

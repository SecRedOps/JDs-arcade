▶ How to play
1
Steer the snake — swipe in any direction on a touchscreen, or use the arrow keys / WASD. The snake never stops moving; you only choose where it turns.
2
Eat the red apples — each one scores a point and makes your snake one segment longer. The snake also speeds up a little with every apple.
3
Grab the gold star when it appears — it's worth 5 points, but it disappears after a few seconds. The shrinking ring shows how long you have.
4
Don't crash — hitting a wall or your own tail ends the game. The longer you get, the harder it is to avoid yourself!

Tip: you can queue your next couple of turns — quick swipes or key presses are remembered in order. Your best score is saved on this device.

Tube Sort

Moves: 0
▶ How to play
1
Pick up a tube — tap any tube that has coloured liquid in it. It will lift up to show it's selected.
2
Pour into another tube — tap a second tube. The liquid pours if the top colour of the destination matches what you're pouring, or the destination is empty.
3
Matching segments move together — if several identical colours sit on top of each other they all pour at once.
4
Win by making every tube hold a single colour from bottom to top (or leaving it empty).

Hint flashes the best next move in yellow/green. Undo reverses your last pour — but costs one move, so use it sparingly!

Try Easy first (6 colours, fewer tubes) and work up to Hard (12 colours) once you're comfortable.

▶ How to play
1
Watch the block slide back and forth above the tower. It never stops on its own — you decide when it lands.
2
Tap anywhere (or press Space) to drop it. Whatever hangs over the edge of the block below gets sliced off and falls away — your tower gets narrower.
3
Land it perfectly (lined up with the block below) and nothing is lost. Chain three perfect drops in a row and your tower grows back wider!
4
Game over when you miss the tower completely. Blocks slide faster the higher you build, so stay sharp.

Scoring: 1 point per block, 2 for a perfect drop. Your best score is saved on this device.

▶ 📲 Install as an app — plays offline

On Android (Chrome):

1
Tap the ⋮ menu in the top-right corner of the browser.
2
Choose “Add to Home screen” — on some phones it says “Install app”.
3
Confirm with Add (or Install). The Arcade icon appears on your home screen.

On iPhone or iPad (Safari):

1
Tap the Share button — the square with an arrow pointing up, at the bottom of the screen.
2
Scroll down the menu and choose “Add to Home Screen”.
3
Tap Add in the top-right corner. The Arcade icon appears on your home screen.

Open it from the icon and the arcade runs full-screen like a normal app — and once it has loaded once, every game works with no internet connection.
